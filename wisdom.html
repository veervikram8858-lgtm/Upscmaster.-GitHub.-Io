House of Wisdom (Bayt al-Hikma)

Return to Portal

1. Foundation & Historical Context

House of Wisdom Baghdad mein sthit ek vishal Library aur Translation Institute tha. Iski sthapna Abbasid Caliph Al-Mansur ne ki thi aur iska 'Golden Era' Caliph Al-Ma'mun (813–833 AD) ke dauran aaya.

Jab Europe mein andhkaar yug (Dark Ages) chal raha tha, tab Baghdad ne Greece, India aur Persia ke gyan ko sanjoya aur uspar aage research ki.

2. The Translation Movement

Iski sabse badi upalabdhi 'The Translation Movement' thi. Duniya bhar ki kitabon ko Arabic mein translate karne ke liye unhe **sone (gold)** ke barabar vajan karke paise diye jate the.

  • Greek Influence: Aristotle aur Plato ke darshan (philosophy) ko bachaya gaya.
  • Persian Influence: Administrative skills aur literature (Shahnama etc.) ko adopt kiya gaya.

Indo-Arab Synthesis (UPSC Special)

House of Wisdom ne Indian knowledge ko West tak pahunchane mein "Bridge" ka kaam kiya. Indian scholars jaise Manka aur Kankah ko Baghdad bulaya gaya tha taaki wo Sanskrit granthon ka Arabic mein anuvad (translation) kar sakein.

3. Major Indian Contributions via Baghdad

Field Indian Source Text Arabic Adaptation / Impact
Mathematics Brahmasphutasiddhanta (Brahmagupta) Arabic mein ise Sindhind kaha gaya. Isse duniya ko 'Zero' aur Decimal system mila.
Medicine Charaka Samhita & Sushruta Samhita Abbasid hospitals mein Indian surgical methods aur herbs ka upyog kiya gaya.
Astronomy Aryabhatiya Baghdad ki observatories ne Indian astronomical calculations ka upyog kiya.

4. Intellectual Giants of the Institute

House of Wisdom ne aise scholars paida kiye jinke bina aadhunik vigyan (modern science) adhura hai:

  • Al-Khwarizmi: Inhe 'Father of Algebra' kaha jata hai. Unka kaam 'Al-Jabr' aaj ke Algebra ka aadhar hai. Unhone hi Indian numerals ko popular banaya.
  • Al-Kindi: Inhe 'Philosopher of the Arabs' kaha jata hai, jinhone Greek philosophy ko Islamic theology ke saath joda.
