Mains Preparation Roadmap

Syllabus Micro-Analysis

GS-1 se GS-4 ke har topic ke liye 2-3 pages ke notes taiyar karein. Static aur Current ko mix karna seekhein.

The 7-Minute Rule

10 marks ke question ko 7 minute mein khatam karne ki aadat dalein. Speed hi Mains nikalne ki chabi hai.

Data & Diagrams

Economic Survey, NITI Aayog reports, aur maps ka use har answer mein karein taaki +1 extra mile.

Essay Likhne Ka Sahi Tarika

Strategy: 1200 Words ka Safar

Essay sirf gyan ka nahi, balki aapke vyaktitv (personality) ka test hai. Iska structure aisa hona chahiye:

  • Brainstorming (15 Mins): Seedha likhna shuru na karein. Rough page par points banayein.
  • Introduction: Kisi story, quote ya heart-touching incident se shuru karein.
  • Multi-Dimensional Body: PESTEL Approach (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal) ka use karein.
  • Balance: Kabhi bhi extreme view na lein. Solutions hamesha constructive honi chahiye.
  • Conclusion: Future-oriented aur optimistic end karein.
