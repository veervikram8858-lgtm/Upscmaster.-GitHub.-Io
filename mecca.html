PAPER I: ANCIENT & MEDIEVAL GEOPOLITICS

Evolution of Mecca: From Tribalism to Empire

1. Political Administration: The 'Mala' System

UPSC ke liye Mecca ka Tribal Oligarchy model samajhna zaroori hai. Yahan koi centralized monarchy nahi thi.

  • The Mala (Council of Elders): Mecca ka prashasan kabilayi sardaron ki ek council chalti thi jise 'Mala' kehte the. Isme har bade kabile ka pratinidhi hota tha, lekin nirnay 'unanimity' (sammati) se liye jate the.
  • Custodianship of Kaaba: Quraish kabila me do mukhya clans the—Hashimites (religious duties) aur Umayyads (political & trade affairs).

Critical Analysis for Mains

"Mecca ki safalta ka raaz uski 'Neutrality' mein tha. Byzantine aur Persians ke yuddh ke beech, Mecca ne khud ko ek 'Zone of Peace' (Haram) banaye rakha, jisne ise international trade ka safe-haven bana diya."

2. Social Stratification & Inequity

Medieval Mecca mein samajik dhancha (Social Structure) kaafi sakht tha. UPSC aspirants ko is 'Social Conflict' par dhyan dena chahiye jo aage chalkar Islam ke uday ka karan bana:

  • Wealth Gap: Trade monopoly ki wajah se amir vyapaari (Merchant Princes) aur garib baduon (Bedouins) ke beech khaayi badh gayi thi.
  • Clientage System (Wala): Jo log kabilo ke sadasya nahi the, unhe 'Mawali' ya clients ban kar rehna padta tha, jinhe purntah adhikaar nahi milte the.

3. Cultural & Intellectual Synthesis

Mecca sirf saman (goods) ka exchange point nahi tha, balki Intellectual Exchange ka bhi kendra tha.

Yahan hone wale 'Ukaz' jaise melaon (fairs) mein kabilayi kavi (poets) apni 'Mu'allaqat' (hangings) pesh karte the. Isse Arabic bhasha ka vikas hua, jo aage chalkar Islamic Science aur Literature ki bhasha bani.

4. Environmental Determinism

Geography ne Mecca ki history ko shape kiya. Kheti ke liye zameen na hone ke karan (Non-agrarian), yahan ki puri arthvyavastha 'Service-based Economy' (Pilgrimage + Trade) par nirbhar thi. UPSC Geography Optional ke liye yeh point mahatvapurn hai.

Prelims Pointer

  • Harram: 20-mile radius around Kaaba where violence was banned.
  • Zamzam: Key reason for Mecca's initial settlement.
  • Ilaf: Diplomatic commercial treaties initiated by Hashim.
Thinking Lab: Think about how Mecca's model of 'Religious Tourism' is similar to modern-day economic hubs that rely on service sectors.
