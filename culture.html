1. Indo-Islamic Architecture: A Synthesis

Medieval India mein architecture sirf building banana nahi tha, balki do mukhya paramparao ka milan tha: Trabeate (Indian) aur Arcuate (Islamic).

Arcuate Style

Islamic influence ne Arches (Mehraab) aur Domes (Gumbad) ka introduction kiya, jisse buildings mein zyada space aur unchai aayi.

Ornamentation

Human figures ki jagah Arabesque (geometrical patterns) aur Calligraphy ka use hua. Saath hi Indian motifs jaise Lotus, Kalash aur Swastika ko bhi apnaya gaya.

Mukhya building materials mein Red Sandstone aur Marble ka prayog hua, aur decoration ke liye Pietra Dura (precious stones inlay work) technique ka vikas hua.

UPSC Mains Insight

"Medieval culture was not a replacement of Indian traditions but a 'Creative Synthesis'. The interaction between the local craftsmen and Central Asian architects led to a unique aesthetic found nowhere else in the Islamic world."

2. The Sufi and Bhakti Movements

Medieval period ka sabse bada cultural impact iske Religious Syncretism mein tha.

  • Sufism: Sufi santon (jaise Nizamuddin Auliya) ne 'Sulh-i-Kul' (Universal Peace) ka sandesh diya aur Music (Qawwali) ko ishwar se milne ka zariya banaya.
  • Bhakti Movement: Saguna aur Nirguna bhakti ne caste barriers ko todne ki koshish ki. Kabir aur Guru Nanak ne ek 'Common Human Brotherhood' ki baat ki.

3. Language & Literature: The Birth of Urdu

Culture ka ek aur bada dimension Linguistic Evolution tha. Persian aur Hindi/Apabhramsha ke mel se Urdu (Lashkari Zaban) ka janm hua. Amir Khusrau ne 'Hindavi' mein kavitaayein likhkar Indian music aur poetry ko naye mukam par pahunchaya.

4. Development of Paintings

Sultanat kal mein manuscripts (Jaise Nimatnama) par painting shuru hui, lekin iska peak Mughal kal mein aaya jahan **Persian Technique** aur **Indian Themes** ka milan hua.

Prelims Focus

  • Double Dome: Introduced by Lodhis (e.g., Humayun's Tomb precursor).
  • True Arch: First seen in Balban's Tomb.
  • Sitar & Tablā: Attributed to Amir Khusrau.
  • Charbagh: Persian style garden layout introduced by Babur.

Key Terms

• Dargah: Shrine built over the grave of a Sufi saint.
