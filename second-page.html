Prophet Mohammad & The Rise of Islam

Medieval history ka ek mahatvapurn hissa 7th century se shuru hota hai jab Arab ke registaan se ek nayi roshni ubhri. Hazrat Mohammad Saheb ka janm 570 AD mein Mecca mein hua tha. Unki shikshao ne na sirf ek naye dharm 'Islam' ko janam diya, balki poore vishva ki rajneetik aur samajik disha badal di.

"Islam ka uday madhyakaaleen itihas (Medieval History) ki sabse krantikari ghatnao mein se ek tha, jisne Asia, Africa aur Europe ke mool-dhanchon ko hamesha ke liye badal diya."

Mohammad Saheb ne ek aise samaj ki neev rakhi jahan samanta (equality) aur ekishwarvaad (monotheism) pradhan tha. 622 AD mein unka Mecca se Medina hijrat (safat) karna 'Hijri Era' ki shuruat bani. Unke baad, Khilafat ke dauran Islam ka vistar tezi se hua, jisne vigyan, kala (art), aur darshan (philosophy) ke naye dwar khole.

MECCA

The spiritual birthplace of Islam and the center of the Medieval world trade.

WISDOM

House of Wisdom in Baghdad became the global hub for scientific learning.

CULTURE

Intricate Islamic architecture that defined the Medieval aesthetics.

Inki vajah se Medieval era mein 'Golden Age' ki shuruat hui, jahan medicine, mathematics aur astronomy mein aisi tarakki hui jo aaj bhi aadhunik vigyan ka aadhar hai.
